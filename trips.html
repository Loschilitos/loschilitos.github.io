Join Our Mission Trip to Rural Guatemala

Make a lasting impact on communities in need while experiencing the beauty of Guatemala.

Why Join Our Mission Trip?

Location: Rural Guatemala

Our mission trip focuses on serving remote communities in Guatemala. You'll work alongside locals in life-changing projects like clean water access, education, and evangelism.

  • Duration: 1-2 weeks
  • Cost: $1,200 (includes airfare, accommodation, and meals)

What You'll Do

As part of the mission team, you'll engage in various outreach activities:

  • Building homes and schools
  • Hosting community health clinics
  • Sharing the Gospel with local families
  • Organizing children's programs

Frequently Asked Questions

What do I need to bring?

You will need a valid passport, clothes suitable for construction and community work, and personal hygiene items. We'll send you a detailed packing list closer to the trip.

Is prior mission experience necessary?

No prior mission experience is required. We welcome anyone with a heart to serve, whether it's your first trip or you're a seasoned mission traveler.

Are there fundraising options?

Yes, we provide fundraising support and ideas to help you raise the necessary funds for the trip.
