About Us

Welcome to Christ Redeemer Church! We are located in rural Guatemala. Specifically in a village called Los chilitos, which belongs to the municipality of Cuilapa, Santa Rosa. We are a member church of the Acts29 Network. Our desire is twofold, that our church would be the center of the community and plant more churches.

Our team desires to know Jesus and make him known. Below, you'll find information about our dedicated team members.

Pastor Cayetano Cardona

Pastor Cayetano has been leading our church with dedication and love since 2021. His inspiring sermons and warm personality make him a cornerstone of our community.

Sarah Smith

Sarah is our assistant and a vital part of our church operations. Her organizational skills and friendly demeanor help keep everything running smoothly.

Mike Johnson

Mike leads our music ministry with passion and creativity. His musical talents and commitment to worship bring joy and inspiration to our services.

Acts 29 Mission Stories: Church Planting in Los Chilitos

As part of our outreach efforts, our church has partnered with organizations like Acts 29 to support missions in remote regions such as Los Chilitos, Guatemala. In this small village, the gospel has been spread through hands-on evangelism, and we’ve had the privilege of helping baptize new believers and witness families and communities being transformed.
